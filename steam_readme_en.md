All-Mode All-Item World, containing every item including v1.4.5 additions.
[b]Please ensure your map is up to date (v3.1.1). If it is not, please update your map.[/b]

[h1]Function:[/h1]
[list]
[*]Orted by item ID; (Using encoding teleporter, [u][url=https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Item_IDs]Item IDs[/url][/u])
[*]Categorized by item type; [b](New!)[/b](New items added in v1.4.5 are marked with red walls)
[*]All npc bestiary; [b](New!)[/b](Book Worm achievement unlocked)
[*]All town NPC rooms;
[*]All workshop;
[*]Nether & Cave spawners & Sky & Surface spawners;
[*]Fully automated tree & gem tree farms;
[/list]
